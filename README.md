# Shield-Insurance-Analysis
I built a Business Performance Dashboard for Shield Insurance using six months data.

## 𝗧𝗮𝘀𝗸 
      🔹I designed a multi-page Power BI dashboard that helps management analyse:
      🔹Revenue and customer trends
      🔹Revenue Split by city and age group
      🔹Sales Modes performance by months, revenue and age group
      🔹Policy Growth and preferences by age group
      🔹Expected Settlement by age group.


## 🔍 Key insights
          🔹Offline Agent remains the strongest sales mode for both customers and revenue, largely driven by the 31–50 age group
          🔹Offline Direct shows a consistent decline, while digital modes (App & Website) show relatively better stability
          🔹Younger customers (18–30) display similar policy preferences and higher digital adoption
          🔹Older age groups expect higher claim settlement percentages, explaining their strong reliance on agent-based sales
          🔹Overall customer growth and stability are primarily driven by the 31–40 segment, making it the core business group.
